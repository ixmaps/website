Welcome to IXmaps

IXmaps: an interactive tool under development designed to permit internet users to see the route that their data packets take across North America, with 'interesting' sites along the way highlighted.


Getting Started

Take a guided tour of selected traceroutes that reveal various interesting features of the North American internet backbone. E.g. where packets pass through suspect sites of NSA warrantless eavesdropping, routes origins and destinations in Canada, that pass through the U.S. where your local Google repositories is located the data centres that contribute most or least to greenhouse gas emissions and more.

Exploring on your Own

Once you have a idea of what IXmaps offers, you can explore on your own by composing your own database queries
Contribute new traceroutes to the database
The database of traceroutes grows through volunteer activity. You can contribute too by installing the special TRgen software and having it generate new traceroutes.
